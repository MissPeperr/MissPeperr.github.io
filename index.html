From a small, farm town in CA, I moved to Nashville in 2015 in the hopes I would be able to find something that I was passionate about in the city. I made new friends, new networks, and worked my way up the ladder at Off Broadway Shoe Warehouse from a Department Supervisor (2016) to Lead Assistant Manager (2017-18). In January of 2018 I had attended a Nashville Software School part-time Jumpstart class where by the end of the 3 weeks I would get a taste for web development and build a website; And I LOVED it. I immediately made plans for attending Nashville Software School, (NSS), full-time and said goodbye to Off Broadway Shoe Warehouse, and said hello to a new career I was fully passionate about.

I graduated the full-time Web Development course at NSS as a Full-Stack Junior Developer in Jan. 2019 and now I work for the very school that introduced me to my love and passion for this career. Every day I look forward to what I'll learn next and how I can utilize it!

My passion for development stems from my love of learning new things. Growing up I was always amazed at how people could do such incredible things; From water coloring and drawing landscapes to building and hosting your own server for certain games (from which there were lessons DEFINITELY learned), I wanted to learn it all. I dove into many subjects before finally landing on one that I was passionate and (most) excited about: technology.

I'm now a mentor to students who made the same decision to change their careers at Nashville Software School. Learning the ins and outs of teaching/coaching is a joy, and I'm excited to bring the same passion to the next team!
